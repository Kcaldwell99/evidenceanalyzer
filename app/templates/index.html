Common questions

Can Evidentix™ tell whether an image was altered?

The platform reviews available metadata, file characteristics, comparison signals, and authenticity indicators, then reports the findings and limits of what can be concluded from the submitted file.

Can it determine ownership?

Ownership conclusions are often limited unless supported by stronger provenance, surrounding facts, or additional investigation. Rights and ownership findings are generally preliminary unless otherwise supported.

What file should I upload?

Upload the original native file whenever possible. Screenshots, texted copies, emailed attachments, and social-media downloads frequently lose important metadata or change file characteristics.

Does buying a report create an attorney-client relationship?

No. Purchase of a report or submission of information does not, by itself, create an attorney-client relationship or legal engagement.

Practical submission tip: If you have both an original file and a copy received by email, text, or social media, preserve and upload both. That can materially improve the usefulness of the analysis.
